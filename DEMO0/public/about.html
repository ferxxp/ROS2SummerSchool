About the Summer School

The iRoboCity2030 Summer School brings together students, researchers, and industry professionals for an intensive programme on ROS 2, Artificial Intelligence, and Field Robotics. Participants gain hands-on experience with real robotic platforms in indoor and outdoor environments.

Preliminary Schedule

WeekTopicFocus
Week 1ROS 2 FoundationsNodes, topics, services, actions, launch files, and debugging tools
Week 2Perception & AICamera drivers, YOLO object detection, depth estimation, sensor fusion
Week 3Navigation & SLAMNav2, Cartographer, RTAB-Map, and autonomous waypoint following
Week 4Field DeploymentMulti-robot coordination, outdoor navigation, and capstone project

Prerequisites

  • Basic proficiency in Python or C++
  • Familiarity with Linux command line
  • Optional: prior exposure to ROS 1 or ROS 2

Location

iRoboCity2030 Laboratory, Campus of the University of Gothenburg, Sweden.

Field sessions will be held at the nearby test track and forest environments.
